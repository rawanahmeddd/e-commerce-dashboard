# E-Commerce Dashboard

A comprehensive analytics and monitoring dashboard for e-commerce operations, providing real-time insights into sales, orders, revenue, and business performance across multiple regions and product categories.

### Dashboard Sections

#### 1. **Sales Analytics**
- **Total Sales by Month**: Line chart tracking monthly sales trends throughout the year
- **Delivery Status**: Breakdown of order statuses including advance shipping, late delivery, shipping canceled, and shipping on time
- **Avg Sales by Segment**: Pie chart showing sales distribution across customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)

#### 2. **Regional Analysis**
- **Total Orders by Region**: Bar chart comparing orders across Central, East, South, and West regions
- **Revenue by Region & Category**: Stacked bar chart displaying revenue distribution for Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology across all regions
- **Total Profit by Top 5 Cities**: Horizontal bar chart highlighting profit leaders (New York City, Los Angeles, Philadelphia, San Francisco, Seattle)

#### 3. **Product Insights**
- **Top 5 Products by Profit**: Data showing highest-performing products including:
  - Staple envelope
  - Staples
  - Easy-staple paper
  - Stoma Data Pro Binders
  - Staples in misc. colors

#### 4. **Category Management**
- **Furniture**: Premium home and office furniture solutions
- **Office Supplies**: Essential office materials and supplies
- **Technology**: High-tech products and gadgets

#### 5. **Time-Based Filtering**
- **Monthly Views**: Interactive filters for January through December
- **Regional Filters**: Central, East, South, West regions
- **Category Filters**: Quick access to product categories

## 📈 Analytics Capabilities

- **Real-time Data**: Monitor sales and orders as they happen
- **Geographic Insights**: Analyze performance by region and city
- **Temporal Analysis**: Track trends across months and seasons
- **Customer Segmentation**: View data by customer type (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- **Delivery Tracking**: Monitor order fulfillment status and identify delays
- **Profit Analysis**: Identify top-performing products and cities

## 🎨 Dashboard Design

The dashboard features:
- **Dark Sidebar Navigation**: Easy-to-use category and time-based filters
- **Interactive Charts**: Click and hover functionality for detailed insights
- **Responsive Layout**: Optimized for desktop and tablet viewing
- **Color-Coded Metrics**: Quick visual identification of key metrics
- **Professional Design**: Clean and modern UI with purple/dark theme

## 📊 Visualization Types

- **Line Charts**: Sales trends over time
- **Bar Charts**: Regional comparisons and city-based analysis
- **Pie Charts**: Segment and category distributions
- **Horizontal Bar Charts**: City rankings and product profitability
- **KPI Cards**: Summary metrics with key statistics
